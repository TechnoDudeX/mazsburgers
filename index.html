How to Make a Maz Burger

Follow these steps and you'll be smashing like a pro in no time.

Ingredients

  • Brioche bun
  • American cheese
  • Burger sauce (mayo, ketchup, mustard, dijon)
  • 2 × 3oz beef patties
  • Maz Burger Seasoning

Tools

  • Burger press
  • Spatula
  • Parchment paper
  • Cast iron skillet
1
Get your cast iron hot and ready. We're talking screaming hot — you want that sear.
2
Take your 2 patty balls and smash them with the burger press using parchment paper. Flat and thin — that's how we get the crispy edges.
3
Lightly season with Maz Burger Seasoning. Don't overdo it — the blend does the work.
4
Flip after 90 seconds, lightly season again, and place the American cheese on one patty. Let it melt.
5
Toast the buns on the cast iron, sauce them up, and stack your masterpiece.
Enjoy 🍔🔥
